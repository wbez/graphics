Chicago binge drinking is concentrated on the North Side

Binge drinking* is more prevalent on the North Side, specifically the Lake View neighborhood, with the CDC estimating more than a third of residents in some areas having done so in the previous 30 days.

Notes

* The CDC defines binge drinking as having five or more drinks for men or four or more drinks for women in the previous 30 days.

Source: CDC 500 Cities project
