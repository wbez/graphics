Firearms recoveries approach peak even as documented stops remain near low point

Notes

* Inventory data not including "weapon turn-in" recoveries such as buybacks. The June 2016 number for firearms recovered is an estimate based on June 1-15 inventory data.
** The data do not include stops of juveniles. Before January 1, 2016, the department did not require officers to submit the report for stops that led to an arrest or citation. After that date, the report has been required for all stops.
