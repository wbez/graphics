    What accessibility looks like under the Fair Housing Act

    Multifamily housing built after March 13, 1991 is subject to certain accessibility requirements under the Fair Housing Act. Here's a look at some of those guidelines.

  • Kitchens and bathrooms with enough space to move around in a wheelchair
  • Lights switches, outlets, and thermostats are accessible
  • Bathrooms have reinforced walls, so grab bars can be installed
  • Public spaces are accessible
  • Accessible entrance
  • Doors wide enough to fit a wheelchair
  • Building can be reached on a route without obstacles or excessive slope
