Timeline

Since 2015, there have been 3 Legionnaires’ outbreaks at the veterans’ home

JULY 2015

The first case of Legionnaires’ disease is found at the Illinois Veterans Home in Quincy.

AUG. - SEPT. 2015

At least 53 people, both residents and staff, are sickened at the veterans’ home. Twelve residents die during this outbreak.

JUNE 29, 2016

“We've got the cleanest water, probably, in the state,” Illinois Department of Veterans' Affairs Director Erica Jeffries tells the Quincy Herald-Whig.

JULY - AUG. 2016

Five more residents test positive for Legionnaires’ disease. “We’re really on top of the situation,” Gov. Bruce Rauner says at a press conference at the facility.

FALL 2017

Three more residents test positive for legionellosis. One of them, a Korean War veteran, dies.
