Illinois’ higher standards

With the addition of the SAT high school exam this year, all Illinois public school students are now being evaluated against relatively high standards. The percentage of Illinois students passing state elementary and high school exams now alligns with Illinois’ performance on NAEP, a rigorous national exam known as the Nation’s Report Card.
