Which Areas Are Gaining, Losing Kids

Since 2000 some Chicago community areas, a stand-in for nieighborhoods, have seen big shifts in the number of people younger than 18. Many African-American communities, such as Austin and South Lawndale, have seen major population loses. Meanwhile, high-income and whiter areas such as Lincoln Park have seen increases.
