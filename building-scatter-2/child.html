In Lake View, demolition makes way for new construction. In Englewood, buildings come down without a plan for what's next.

Community areas higher on the chart have a larger amount of new construction permits, those further to the right have a larger number of demolition permits. The Loop sees lots of new construction and little demolition. Englewood and West Englewood have lots of demolition activity but alnost no new construction. Lake View has a great amount of both.
