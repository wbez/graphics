Number of top-rated Chicago schools triples over five years

CPS changed how it rates schools to emphasize student growth on standardized tests instead of absolute performance, pushing more schools into higher tiers.

Notes

In 2011, CPS schools earned one of three ratings: Level 1, Level 2 or Level 3. By 2016, CPS had moved to five levels: 1+, 1, 2+, 2, and 3.

Source: Chicago Public Schools
